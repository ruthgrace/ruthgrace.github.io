breathable underwear for pregnant women

no more swamp crotch!

This is the designer rendering of the breathable underwear made by Kaixi Yang.

For my first pregnancy, the only underwear that was comfortable was my worn-thin cotton underwear that I'd had since high school. But now I'm pregnant again and my old underwear is developing holes! I am developing underwear that is:


* Made from wool. Nothing beats wool for durability and breathability. Its scaley microsurface allows moisture to evaporate away faster than on cotton.


* No bulky elastic. I don't want to irritate my C-section scar.


* No bulky seams for maximum comfort


* Fits under a pregnant belly
